About the Group

Who We Are

Ain Al Khaleej Group is a leading UAE-based institution dedicated to advancing human potential through world-class healthcare and education. Founded in Al Ain, the cultural heart of Abu Dhabi, the Group embodies the values of trust, compassion, and excellence that define the Emirates.

With decades of service to the community, our institutions stand as landmarks of quality — shaping healthier lives, brighter futures, and stronger communities across the region.
